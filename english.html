💡 How gachapon works (quick tips)

Price: mostly ¥300–¥500 per turn, shown on each machine's front panel. Premium series can be ¥1,000+.

Coins & change: machines take ¥100 coins. Specialty stores have change machines; smaller corners in stations or supermarkets often don't — carry coins. Newer stores increasingly accept Suica/PASMO or QR pay.

Etiquette: queue for popular machines, turn the crank slowly all the way, and drop empty capsules in the recycle box you'll find in most stores.

Language: the rest of this site is in Japanese, but it works well with your browser's translate function. Every store page has an address and a map, which work in any language.
